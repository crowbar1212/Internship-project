Charts and Reports

How do I create a new measure?
Select STEP 1 and follow the instructions
Can I change the name of an existing department or an existing measure?
Yes - please email Greg Trader and he will take care of it
How do I save my data?
Whenever a change is made an image of a disk will appear in the upper right part of the screen. Click the image and read.
Do I need to fill out the analysis section in STEP 6?
Yes.
Can I export the charts that appear in STEPS 2-6?
Yes - Hover over the chart and you will see an icon that looks like a camera. Click this icon and a PNG file will be created.
I see report but no charts...what gives?
Some will be established shortly
My summary chart on STEP 2 only has a target (red) line. Is something missing?
Yes! This chart is supposed to show the target compared to the KPI (Key Performance Indicator). The KPI can be assigned or modified in STEP 4 under the "Which number is being compared against the target value?" section
